Feedback Form

Being a guitar player, you know that feedback can be a great thing! That being said, it can be difficult to find honest, supportive, and productive critique about your guitar playing. That's why I created this service on ckblues.com. Here's how it works:

  1. Create a video of yourself playing guitar that demonstrates what you need help with.
  2. Upload it to Youtube.
  3. Copy the Youtube share link to your video.
  4. Paste the link bellow and fill out the rest of the form.

I will review your video and any notes you've submitted. After that, you will receive a personalized video screencast (aka feedback) to help improve your guitar playing.

Maybe you have a question on scale choice over the blues? Having an issue with barre chords? Whatever question you have, record yourself playing to demonstrate, and I'll steer you in the right direction.
